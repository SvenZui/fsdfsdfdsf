Let's learn spells

Wingardium Leviosa

A levitation spell that causes an object to fly.

Incendio

Causes fire to fly from the stick.

Petrificus Totalus

Spell-body binding

Locomotive Mortis

A spell that binds the victim's legs and thereby prevents her from walking.

Alogomora

A spell that unlocks and opens normal doors, windows, etc.

Rictumsepra

A spell that sends an unbearable tickle to the spelled.
